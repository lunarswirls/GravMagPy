## Methodology Differences and Why Pre-Collective Residuals Were Larger

- Pre-collective workflow solved bodies in a less coupled way before assembling total response.
- SHTOOLS reference is a global SH LSQ field representation from the final sampled field.

Why this increased mismatch:

- Independent body treatment can produce coefficient partitioning inconsistencies when anomalies overlap spatially.
- Summation after separate solves does not enforce a single global coefficient optimum.
- Tangential components are especially sensitive to these partitioning differences.

The later collective-fit implementation reduces this inconsistency by enforcing one global solve across all bodies.
